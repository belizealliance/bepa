    Mission Statement

    The Belize Environmental Protection Alliance seeks to unite activists, journalists, students, NGOs, and businesses across Belize in the fight for sustainable development and environmental protection. The alliance will serve as a resource for individuals across to Belize to find support for their activism and for people around the world to stay up to date on Belize environmental news.
